## Bang phan quyen tac nhan

Sau khi xac dinh cac tac nhan chinh cua he thong, viec phan quyen chuc nang giup lam ro pham vi thao tac cua tung nhom nguoi dung. He thong DMS duoc thiet ke theo huong moi nguoi dung chi duoc truy cap vao cac chuc nang phu hop voi vai tro va pham vi du lieu duoc giao. Cach tiep can nay giup dam bao tinh bao mat, tranh viec nguoi dung thao tac nham du lieu ngoai trach nhiem, dong thoi ho tro qua trinh quan ly tap trung cua doanh nghiep.

| Nhom chuc nang | Quan tri vien | Nha phan phoi | Nhan vien ban hang |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dang nhap, dang xuat, doi mat khau | Co | Co | Co |
| Quan ly nguoi dung | Co | Xem doi DSR thuoc quyen | Khong |
| Quan ly khach hang | Co | Co trong pham vi phu trach | Co voi khach hang cua minh |
| Duyet khach hang | Co | Co theo pham vi | Khong |
| Quan ly san pham va danh muc | Co | Xem san pham | Xem san pham duoc ban |
| Quan ly kho hang | Co | Quan ly kho nha phan phoi | Xem ton kho phuc vu ban hang |
| Quan ly don hang | Co | Co trong pham vi phu trach | Tao va theo doi don cua minh |
| Quan ly tuyen ban hang | Co | Tao va theo doi tuyen cua doi | Xem tuyen duoc giao |
| Check-in/check-out ghe tham | Theo doi | Theo doi doi DSR | Thuc hien tren ung dung di dong |

Bang phan quyen tren cho thay quan tri vien la tac nhan co quyen quan ly tong the, nha phan phoi tap trung vao du lieu cua doi ngu phu trach, con nhan vien ban hang chu yeu thuc hien cac nghiep vu phat sinh ngoai thi truong. Viec gioi han pham vi thao tac theo vai tro giup he thong phu hop voi cau truc van hanh cua doanh nghiep phan phoi.

## Phan tich cac ca su dung chinh

### Ca su dung dang nhap he thong

Ca su dung dang nhap cho phep nguoi dung truy cap vao he thong bang tai khoan duoc cap. Nguoi dung nhap thong tin email va mat khau, sau do he thong gui yeu cau den backend de xac thuc. Neu thong tin hop le, he thong tra ve thong tin nguoi dung, access token va refresh token. Dua tren vai tro cua nguoi dung, giao dien se hien thi cac chuc nang phu hop.

Trong truong hop thong tin dang nhap khong dung, he thong hien thi thong bao loi ro rang de nguoi dung co the kiem tra lai. Neu tai khoan bi khoa hoac khong con hoat dong, he thong khong cho phep dang nhap. Ca su dung nay la diem bat dau cua hau het cac luong nghiep vu khac, dong thoi la co che quan trong de bao ve du lieu trong he thong.

### Ca su dung quan ly khach hang

Chuc nang quan ly khach hang duoc su dung de luu tru va cap nhat thong tin diem ban. Nhan vien ban hang co the tao moi khach hang tren ung dung di dong khi phat sinh diem ban moi ngoai thi truong. Thong tin khach hang bao gom ten khach hang, ten chu cua hang, so dien thoai, dia chi, loai khach hang va toa do vi tri neu co.

Sau khi khach hang duoc tao, he thong co the dua khach hang vao trang thai cho duyet. Quan tri vien hoac nha phan phoi kiem tra thong tin va thuc hien duyet hoac tu choi. Khi khach hang duoc duyet, nhan vien ban hang co the su dung khach hang do trong cac nghiep vu tiep theo nhu lap tuyen, check-in va tao don hang.

Chuc nang nay giup doanh nghiep quan ly tap trung danh sach diem ban, han che trung lap thong tin va dam bao chi cac khach hang hop le moi duoc tham gia vao quy trinh ban hang.

### Ca su dung quan ly tuyen ban hang

Quan ly tuyen ban hang cho phep quan tri vien hoac nha phan phoi lap ke hoach lam viec cho nhan vien ban hang. Moi tuyen co cac thong tin nhu ten tuyen, ngay lam viec, nhan vien phu trach, danh sach khach hang can ghe tham va thu tu thuc hien.

Khi tuyen duoc tao, nhan vien ban hang co the xem danh sach tuyen duoc giao tren ung dung di dong. Trong qua trinh lam viec, nhan vien thuc hien check-in tai tung diem ban va he thong cap nhat trang thai cua diem ban trong tuyen. Nha phan phoi va quan tri vien co the theo doi tien do thuc hien tuyen thong qua du lieu ghe tham duoc ghi nhan.

Chuc nang tuyen ban hang giup hoat dong thi truong co ke hoach hon, dong thoi tao co so de danh gia muc do hoan thanh cong viec cua nhan vien ban hang.

### Ca su dung check-in/check-out ghe tham

Check-in/check-out la chuc nang cot loi cua ung dung di dong DMS Seller. Khi nhan vien ban hang den diem ban, nhan vien chon khach hang hoac diem trong tuyen, sau do thuc hien check-in. Ung dung lay vi tri hien tai cua thiet bi va gui cac thong tin gom khach hang, tuyen neu co, thoi gian, vi do, kinh do va ghi chu len backend.

Khi ket thuc qua trinh lam viec tai diem ban, nhan vien thuc hien check-out. He thong tiep tuc ghi nhan thoi gian ket thuc, vi tri check-out va ghi chu neu co. Du lieu nay giup nha quan ly biet duoc nhan vien da ghe diem ban nao, vao thoi diem nao va hoan thanh cong viec ra sao.

Trong thuc te, chuc nang nay can xu ly cac truong hop nhu chua cap quyen vi tri, GPS yeu, khong lay duoc toa do hoac nhan vien dang co mot luot ghe tham chua ket thuc. Viec kiem soat cac dieu kien nay giup du lieu ghe tham co tinh chinh xac va phan anh dung hoat dong ngoai thi truong.

### Ca su dung tao va quan ly don hang

Chuc nang don hang cho phep nhan vien ban hang tao don truc tiep tren ung dung di dong sau khi lam viec voi khach hang. Nhan vien chon khach hang, chon san pham, nhap so luong, ap dung khuyen mai neu co va nhap ghi chu don hang. He thong tinh tong tien, giam gia va gia tri thanh toan cua don hang.

Sau khi don hang duoc tao, quan tri vien hoac nha phan phoi co the theo doi va xu ly don theo quy trinh nghiep vu. Don hang co the trai qua cac trang thai nhu cho duyet, da duyet, da giao, yeu cau tra hang, da huy hoac da tra hang. Khi don hang duoc giao, he thong co the cap nhat ton kho va ghi nhan cac thong tin lien quan den doanh thu, cong no va loi nhuan.

Chuc nang quan ly don hang giup doanh nghiep theo doi toan bo qua trinh ban hang tu khi phat sinh nhu cau tai diem ban den khi giao hang va thanh toan. Day la mot trong nhung du lieu quan trong nhat phuc vu bao cao va danh gia hieu qua kinh doanh.

### Ca su dung thanh toan va cong no

Thanh toan va cong no giup he thong ghi nhan so tien khach hang da thanh toan cho don hang. Nguoi dung co the cap nhat so tien thanh toan, phuong thuc thanh toan va ghi chu lien quan. He thong dua vao tong gia tri don hang va so tien da thu de xac dinh trang thai thanh toan nhu chua thanh toan, thanh toan mot phan hoac da thanh toan.

Trong truong hop phat sinh hoan tien hoac tra hang, he thong can cap nhat lai so tien da thu, so tien con lai va trang thai cong no. Cac du lieu nay giup bo phan quan ly theo doi dong tien, kiem soat cac khoan phai thu va danh gia tinh hinh tai chinh cua hoat dong ban hang.

### Ca su dung quan ly KPI

Chuc nang KPI duoc su dung de theo doi muc tieu va ket qua thuc hien cua nhan vien ban hang. KPI co the bao gom doanh thu muc tieu, doanh thu thuc te, so don hang muc tieu, so don hang thuc te, so luot ghe tham muc tieu va so luot ghe tham thuc te trong thang.

Nhan vien ban hang co the xem KPI ca nhan tren ung dung di dong de biet muc do hoan thanh cong viec. Nha phan phoi va quan tri vien co the theo doi KPI cua doi ngu nhan vien de danh gia hieu qua lam viec. Viec gan KPI voi du lieu thuc te tu don hang va luot ghe tham giup qua trinh danh gia co co so ro rang hon.

### Ca su dung thong bao

Thong bao giup he thong gui cac cap nhat quan trong den nguoi dung. Cac thong bao co the lien quan den don hang, tuyen ban hang, nghi phep, khach hang, kho hang, khuyen mai hoac cac noi dung he thong. Nguoi dung co the xem danh sach thong bao, mo chi tiet thong bao va danh dau da doc.

Chuc nang thong bao giup nguoi dung nam bat su kien moi kip thoi, dac biet trong cac tinh huong can phan hoi nhanh nhu don hang moi, thay doi trang thai, duyet nghi phep hoac phan cong tuyen ban hang. Doi voi ung dung di dong, thong bao gop phan giam viec nguoi dung phai kiem tra thu cong tung man hinh.

## Quy trinh nghiep vu tieu bieu

### Quy trinh tao khach hang va duyet khach hang

Quy trinh tao khach hang bat dau khi nhan vien ban hang phat hien mot diem ban moi. Nhan vien mo ung dung DMS Seller, nhap thong tin khach hang va gui len he thong. Backend luu thong tin khach hang moi va gan trang thai cho duyet neu quy trinh yeu cau kiem tra truoc khi su dung.

Sau do, quan tri vien hoac nha phan phoi truy cap giao dien web de xem danh sach khach hang dang cho duyet. Neu thong tin hop le, nguoi quan ly thuc hien duyet khach hang. Neu thong tin chua day du hoac khong phu hop, nguoi quan ly co the tu choi va nhap ly do. Ket qua duyet duoc cap nhat ve he thong va nhan vien ban hang co the theo doi trang thai tren ung dung.

Quy trinh nay giup doanh nghiep kiem soat chat luong du lieu khach hang, tranh viec dua cac diem ban sai thong tin vao quy trinh ban hang.

### Quy trinh tuyen ban hang, ghe tham va tao don

Quy trinh tuyen ban hang bat dau khi quan tri vien hoac nha phan phoi lap tuyen cho nhan vien ban hang. Tuyen bao gom ngay lam viec va danh sach khach hang can ghe tham theo thu tu. Khi den ngay lam viec, nhan vien ban hang mo ung dung de xem tuyen duoc giao.

Tai moi diem ban, nhan vien thuc hien check-in de ghi nhan thoi gian va vi tri. Sau khi trao doi voi khach hang, neu phat sinh nhu cau mua hang, nhan vien tao don hang truc tiep tren ung dung. Don hang duoc gui ve backend va xuat hien tren giao dien quan ly cua nha phan phoi hoac quan tri vien.

Sau khi hoan thanh cong viec tai diem ban, nhan vien check-out. Trang thai luot ghe tham va tien do tuyen duoc cap nhat. Nha quan ly co the theo doi so diem da ghe, so diem con lai va cac don hang phat sinh trong ngay.

Quy trinh nay the hien moi lien ket giua lap ke hoach, thuc thi ngoai thi truong va quan ly don hang. Day cung la luong nghiep vu trung tam cua he thong DMS.

### Quy trinh xu ly don hang va cap nhat kho

Sau khi don hang duoc tao, don o trang thai cho xu ly hoac cho duyet tuy theo quy dinh nghiep vu. Nha phan phoi hoac quan tri vien kiem tra thong tin don hang, bao gom khach hang, san pham, so luong, gia ban, khuyen mai va ghi chu. Neu don hop le, nguoi quan ly duyet don va thuc hien cac buoc giao hang.

Khi don hang duoc giao thanh cong, he thong cap nhat trang thai don hang sang da giao. Dong thoi, so luong ton kho lien quan co the duoc tru tu kho phu hop. Neu don hang thuoc seller cua nha phan phoi, gia ban co the duoc lay theo gia ban tai kho nha phan phoi. He thong cung co the ghi nhan gia von, doanh thu, loi nhuan va cong no phat sinh.

Trong truong hop don hang bi huy hoac tra hang, he thong cap nhat lai trang thai don va xu ly cac du lieu lien quan den ton kho, thanh toan va cong no. Quy trinh nay giup dam bao du lieu don hang va kho hang duoc dong bo voi nhau.

### Quy trinh nghi phep

Nhan vien ban hang co the tao don nghi phep tren ung dung di dong bang cach chon ngay bat dau, ngay ket thuc va nhap ly do nghi. Don nghi sau khi gui len he thong se o trang thai cho duyet. Nha phan phoi hoac quan tri vien xem danh sach don nghi va thuc hien duyet hoac tu choi.

Neu don nghi duoc duyet, nhan vien ban hang co the xem trang thai moi tren ung dung. Neu don bi tu choi, he thong hien thi ly do de nhan vien nam thong tin. Quy trinh nghi phep giup doanh nghiep quan ly lich lam viec cua nhan vien ban hang va co co so dieu chinh tuyen ban hang khi can thiet.

## Thiet ke du lieu muc tong quan

He thong DMS su dung co so du lieu MongoDB de luu tru du lieu nghiep vu. MongoDB phu hop voi he thong vi du lieu co nhieu nhom doi tuong khac nhau, co quan he linh hoat va can kha nang mo rong theo tung module. Cac collection chinh cua he thong co the bao gom users, customers, products, categories, warehouses, warehouse_stocks, inventory_transactions, promotions, routes, visits, orders, leaves, notifications, kpis va audit_logs.

Collection users luu thong tin tai khoan nguoi dung, vai tro, trang thai hoat dong va quan he quan ly. Vai tro nguoi dung la co so de backend kiem tra quyen truy cap va gioi han du lieu tra ve cho tung nguoi dung.

Collection customers luu thong tin khach hang va diem ban. Moi khach hang co cac thong tin nhu ten, so dien thoai, dia chi, toa do, nguoi phu trach, loai khach hang va trang thai duyet. Du lieu khach hang duoc su dung trong cac nghiep vu tuyen ban hang, ghe tham va don hang.

Collection products va categories luu thong tin san pham va nhom san pham. San pham co cac truong nhu ma san pham, ten san pham, don vi tinh, gia, ton kho co ban, hinh anh va trang thai kinh doanh. Cac thong tin nay duoc su dung khi tao don hang va theo doi doanh thu.

Collection warehouses, warehouse_stocks va inventory_transactions phuc vu quan ly kho. Warehouses luu thong tin kho, warehouse_stocks luu so luong ton theo tung san pham trong tung kho, con inventory_transactions ghi nhan lich su nhap, xuat va dieu chinh ton kho. Nhom du lieu nay giup he thong theo doi hang hoa trong qua trinh phan phoi.

Collection routes luu ke hoach tuyen ban hang, bao gom nhan vien phu trach, ngay lam viec, danh sach khach hang trong tuyen va trang thai tung diem. Collection visits luu cac luot ghe tham thuc te cua nhan vien ban hang, gom thoi gian check-in/check-out, toa do va ghi chu. Hai collection nay lien ket chat che de phan anh ke hoach va ket qua thuc hien ngoai thi truong.

Collection orders luu thong tin don hang, danh sach san pham, tong tien, giam gia, trang thai don, thanh toan, cong no va loi nhuan neu co. Don hang co lien ket voi khach hang va nhan vien ban hang, dong thoi co lien quan den kho khi don duoc giao.

Collection leaves, notifications, kpis va audit_logs ho tro cac chuc nang bo sung. Leaves luu don nghi phep, notifications luu thong bao gui den nguoi dung, kpis luu muc tieu va ket qua thuc hien, con audit_logs ghi nhan cac thao tac quan trong trong he thong. Cac collection nay giup he thong hoan thien hon ve mat quan ly, theo doi va kiem soat.

## Ket luan phan phan tich

Qua qua trinh phan tich yeu cau, tac nhan, ca su dung va du lieu, co the thay he thong DMS duoc xay dung nham giai quyet bai toan quan ly phan phoi theo huong tap trung va dong bo. He thong khong chi ho tro bo phan quan ly theo doi du lieu tren web, ma con cung cap ung dung di dong cho nhan vien ban hang lam viec truc tiep ngoai thi truong.

Cac chuc nang nhu quan ly khach hang, tuyen ban hang, check-in/check-out, don hang, kho hang, thanh toan, KPI va thong bao co moi lien ket chat che voi nhau. Du lieu phat sinh tu mot nghiep vu co the duoc su dung cho nhieu muc dich khac nhau nhu theo doi tien do, xu ly don hang, tinh doanh thu, cap nhat ton kho va danh gia hieu qua lam viec.

Phan tich tren la co so de thiet ke kien truc he thong, thiet ke co so du lieu, xay dung giao dien va trien khai cac module chuc nang trong cac phan tiep theo cua bao cao.
